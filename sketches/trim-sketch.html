Cuts vs current
  • Eyebrow

  • "Source sheet URL" field label
  • "Standby" idle badge
  • "? Tour" + "↗ Float" pills (now in ⋯ menu)
  • Settings link in footer (also in ⋯)

What stays the same in every variant: Step rail, mode toggle, Slow toggle, sheet input, coachmark "?" pill, sharing pill, Connect CTA, version footer. The cuts are all chrome, not function.
B vs C: Only difference is whether the descriptive paragraph stays. If you trust the title alone, go C. If the paragraph helps first-time users, go B.
C vs D: D goes further by collapsing the masthead pills into a ⋯ menu and dropping the poetic title. Less personality, more density.
If unsure: Pick C. It's the cleanest cut that still feels like the rest of the popup.
