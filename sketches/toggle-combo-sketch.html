Of the two ways to keep the "for who" hint: B-2 wins on three counts — (1) the caption sits directly under the pill it explains, so the relationship is unambiguous; (2) it's one clean line instead of B-1's two-line wrap; (3) it places best for screen readers (caption follows the pill in DOM order, can be tied via aria-describedby).
