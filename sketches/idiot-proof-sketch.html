Idiot-proof sketches — fixing the sheet-sharing block

Four ways to stop colleagues from getting stuck on "I don't want my sheet public." Each variant centers the same escape hatch: make a throwaway sheet, share that one, don't touch your real one. All show a popup mock at its critical moment.

The actual breakage: Samuel (and others) refuse to set their working sheet to "Anyone with link · Editor." They don't realise they can just spin up a fresh blank sheet, scrape into it, then copy the rows out. Every variant below makes this option physically impossible to miss.

Pick one — or mix & match

The four variants aren't mutually exclusive. A common stack: C (onboarding) + B (coachmarks) = one-time hand-holding plus contextual help forever. A stricter stack: D (checklist) + B (coachmarks). The nuclear option: A (full wizard) on its own.
